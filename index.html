Top Picks For You!
Taj Mahal
An Immortal Symbol of Love
Majestically located on the banks of Yamuna River, this wonder of the world was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan as a memorial for his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal.


Le ROI Floating Huts & Eco Rooms, Tehri, Uttarakhand
Lavish Gardens | Classy Rooms | Farm to Table Experience
Book a regal stay at Tijara Palace that features opulent rooms with royal interiors. Savour organic food in alfresco dining and explore the lavish outdoors..

Alsisar Mahal, A Heritage Hotel, Jhunjhunu
Rajputana Decor | Rooftop Restaurant |Camel Safari
Enjoy a heritage stay as you dig into a fabulous gourmet experience, head for camel safaris and sign up for a demo cooking class.


Sitabani Reserve Forest
For Amazing Trekking Trails
Sitabani Forest has some of the finest trekking trails, where you can walk through the dense jungle and discover its mysteries. The walk takes you close to the tiger reserve.


Itsy Bitsy Cabin, Kasol
Dome Shaped Cabin | Apple Orchards | Stargazing| Kitchenette
Book your starry nights in this beautiful glass dome, away from the city’s hustle! Bask in the sunny apple orchards and cook delicious meals!



Siddha Wellness Village, Faridabad
Spa | Stocked Kitchenette| Indoor &Outdoor Games| Garden
Escape into the lap of luxury as you relax with rejuvenating therapies, enjoy few laps in the pool, stroll in the lawn and match wits over many games.


Witness the grandeur of Dholpur Palace
Admire the Intricate Architecture
Also known as Raj Niwas Palace, Dholpur Palace is spread across 13 acres of land, and was built in the 19th century.




Siliserh Lake
19th Century Lake
Built in 1848 by Maharaja Vinay Singh, Siliserh Lake was dedicated to his queen, Sheela. It has two earthen dams and the Aravalli hills as its backdrop.
